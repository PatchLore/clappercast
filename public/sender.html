Step 1 — Connect your phone
Open on phone: loading…

Make sure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi as this PC.

Step 2 — OBS Setup
In OBS Studio Audio Settings → Monitoring Device → set to VB-Audio Virtual Cable.
Then on each audio source: Advanced Audio → Monitor and Output.
Then here Select CABLE Output (VB-Audio) as your audio source above, then hit Start.
On Clapper Open Clapper Radio on your phone — it will pick up this audio as if it's a wired headset. 🎙️
Step 3 — Go live
Latency target: <50ms on local Wi-Fi

Once the WebRTC stream is connected, open Clapper Radio on your phone and start a session. Clapper will use the audio routed from this PC as its mic input. You control the mix here — Clapper sees it as a "wired headset."
