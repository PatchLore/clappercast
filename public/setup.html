Get set up in 5 minutes — follow every step or it won't work

Use this checklist exactly in order. Most "no audio" issues come from skipping one setup step.

SECTION 1 — Windows PC Setup (do this once)

1

Download VB-Audio Virtual Cable (free)

  • Go to vb-audio.com/Cable
  • Download and install
⚠️ RESTART YOUR PC after installing — it won't work without a restart.
Screenshot placeholder: VB-Audio download page + installer
2

Set your audio output correctly

This is the most common mistake.

  • Open the app you want to stream (Spotify, OBS, YouTube etc)
  • Right-click the Windows speaker icon in taskbar → Open Volume Mixer
  • Find your app in the list
  • Change its output to CABLE Input (VB-Audio Virtual Cable)
⚠️ If you skip this step you will hear silence on your phone.
Screenshot placeholder: Windows Volume Mixer with CABLE Input selected
3

Open ClapperCast on your PC

  • Open Chrome (not Edge, not Firefox)
  • Go to https://[your-domain]/
  • Chrome will warn about the certificate — click Advanced → Proceed
  • Allow microphone access when prompted
  • Select CABLE Output (VB-Audio Virtual Cable) from the dropdown
Screenshot placeholder: sender page with CABLE Output selected

SECTION 2 — iPhone Setup (do this once)

4

Trust the certificate on iPhone

  • Open Safari on your iPhone
  • Go to https://[your-domain]/receiver
  • Tap Show Details → Visit this website → Visit Website
  • If prompted: Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → Trust
Screenshot placeholder: Safari certificate trust flow
5

Allow audio

  • Tap Tap to receive audio — you MUST tap this before audio will play
  • Make sure your iPhone is NOT on silent mode (check the switch on the left side)
  • Turn volume up using the physical buttons
Screenshot placeholder: receiver page after audio unlock tap

SECTION 3 — Going live on Clapper

⚠️ Enable Do Not Disturb on your iPhone before going live — notifications will interrupt your audio
6

Connect everything

  • PC: select CABLE Output, click Start Streaming
  • Phone: confirm it shows You are live
  • Check the visualiser bars are moving — if they're flat your audio routing is wrong (go back to Step 2)
7

Open Clapper Radio

  • Open the Clapper app
  • Tap + → Radio → Start
  • Clapper picks up your PC audio as the microphone
⚠️ Keep the ClapperCast browser tab open in the background on your phone.

SECTION 4 — Troubleshooting

Problem Fix
Visualiser bars not moving Check Step 2 — your app output is wrong.
No sound on phone Check silent mode switch and volume.
Phone can't connect Make sure phone and PC are on same Wi-Fi.
Start button greyed out Allow microphone in Chrome (click padlock icon).
Certificate warning on iPhone Follow Step 4 again.
Audio cuts out Keep phone screen on, disable low power mode.
