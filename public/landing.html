How it works

Step 1

Open the ClapperCast Bridge sender on your PC and connect your VB-Audio cable or built-in mic.

Step 2

Scan the QR code with your phone browser (no install) and tap “receive audio”.

Step 3

Go live on Clapper, TikTok, or IG — your phone hears the PC audio like a wired headset.
